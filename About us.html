About us

It’s quite hard to save these days. And it’s even harder to look for coupons in order to save. In fact, it is one of those tedious tasks you carry out. But, guess what? Your worries are over as we introduce Slash.Live!

Slash.Live is a fresh-out-of-the-oven platform where we collect all the coupons from all sorts of categories over thousands of brands just for you! No more hard work wasting hours and hours searching for that perfect coupon. We got you!

Our goal is to find you the perfect coupon and connect you with your favorite brand. On top of that, with our integrated AI extension linked to your browser, you will get real-time updates from any coupon which is uploaded anywhere among our partner brands.
